About Amir'O Graphy

Welcome to Amir'O Graphy, a platform dedicated to photographers and visual storytellers. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where artists can share their work, gain exposure, and monetize their creativity while connecting with like-minded individuals.

Subscription Plans

We offer different membership levels to suit your needs:

  • Free Members: Can upload 2 photos per day and download 1 photo daily
  • Explorer ($2.99/month): Upload 5 photos per day and download 5 photos daily
  • Unlimited ($7.99/month): Unlimited uploads and downloads, plus premium features

Monetization Program

Our monetization program allows photographers to earn from their work:

  • Free Members: Need 100 likes and 20 comments to apply
  • Subscribers: Need 50 likes and 10 comments to apply
  • After approval, you can set prices for your photos (subject to admin review)
  • Earnings are paid out monthly via your preferred method

Copyright Guidelines

All photographs displayed on this platform are the intellectual property of their respective creators. Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution of these images without explicit permission is strictly prohibited.

When you purchase a photo through Amir'O Graphy, you're acquiring specific usage rights as outlined in our licensing agreements. Full copyright transfer requires a separate agreement with the photographer.

Community Guidelines

We encourage respectful engagement within our community. Please:

  • Credit original photographers when sharing their work
  • Respect copyright and licensing terms
  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Report any inappropriate content

For licensing inquiries, copyright issues, or monetization questions, please contact us at ahamed.to.tipak@gmail.com.
